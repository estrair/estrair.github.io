About EstrAir

EstrAir is focused on addressing airway degeneration associated with hormonal changes in menopause.

Despite the prevalence of voice changes, airway dryness, chronic cough, and sleep-disordered breathing in midlife women, the airway remains an understudied and undertreated target of therapy.

EstrAir is exploring research pathways aimed at understanding these changes and identifying potential therapeutic strategies.
