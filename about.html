About EstrAir

EstrAir is focused on addressing airway degeneration associated with hormonal changes in menopause.

Despite the prevalence of voice changes, airway dryness, chronic cough, and sleep-disordered breathing in midlife women, the airway remains an understudied and undertreated target of therapy.

EstrAir is developing a new approach to women’s health focused on the airway — an overlooked system affected by hormonal changes during menopause.

Many women experience persistent dry cough, voice changes, airway dryness, and sleep disruption during midlife, yet these symptoms are rarely connected to underlying biological changes in airway tissue.

EstrAir is exploring targeted, localized strategies to better understand and address these symptoms at their source.

Investigational only. Not FDA approved.
