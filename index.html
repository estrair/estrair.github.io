EstrAir

Advancing airway-focused therapies to improve sleep health

The Problem

Millions of people suffer from obstructive sleep apnea and other airway-related sleep disorders. Current treatments often focus on managing symptoms rather than addressing underlying airway physiology and structural contributors to disease.

Our Approach

EstrAir is focused on developing innovative airway-focused approaches aimed at improving sleep outcomes. Our work explores mechanisms underlying airway collapse and obstruction during sleep and seeks to develop therapies that target these root causes.

Scientific Collaboration

EstrAir is currently engaging with researchers and clinicians specializing in sleep medicine, upper airway physiology, and respiratory health to evaluate potential research pathways and clinical opportunities.
