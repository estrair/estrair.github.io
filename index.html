EstrAir

A new approach to cough, voice changes, and disrupted sleep

Menopause can bring unexpected airway symptoms — persistent dry cough, voice changes, throat dryness, and disrupted sleep.

EstrAir is developing the first targeted approach to treat these symptoms at the source: the airway itself.

The Problem

Millions of women experience airway-related symptoms during menopause that are often overlooked or misattributed.

  • Chronic dry cough
  • Voice changes or fatigue
  • Throat and airway dryness
  • Sleep disruption and breathing changes
