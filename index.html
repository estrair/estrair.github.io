The Problem

Many women experience persistent airway symptoms during menopause that are often overlooked, misdiagnosed, or dismissed.

  • Chronic dry cough
  • Voice changes or fatigue
  • Throat and airway dryness
  • Sleep disruption and breathing changes

An under-recognized area of women’s health with no targeted therapies today.
