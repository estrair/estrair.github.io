Scientific Focus

EstrAir focuses on the intersection of airway physiology, hormonal biology, and sleep health — an area that has been consistently under-researched and underserved in women's medicine.

  • Hormonal influences on airway tissue structure and function.
  • Changes in upper airway integrity associated with menopause.
  • Implications for voice, breathing, and sleep quality.
  • Exploration of localized therapeutic approaches.

This work is exploratory and aims to better understand an under-recognized aspect of women's health. EstrAir is in the research and development phase. No products are currently available for clinical use.
